About Us

Modern Design Carpentry tailor solutions to our customers specific needs inside and outside of the home.

We focus on bespoke made to measure: wardrobes, alcove units, Tv units, kitchens and any custom built furniture.

We also build outdoor projects: garden fencing, custom seating, office pods.

"Our products are made to measure, designed to suit your needs and your style.

While we can offer you the ideas we have, and make suggestions based on our experience, we like hearing and seing examples of what you would like to see in a finished product, and bring your idea to life ensuring you can get exactly what you have in mind."
